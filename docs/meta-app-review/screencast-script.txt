Screencast script for Meta App Review (documents-web-1)
========================================================
Target length: under 2 minutes. Record as .mp4 or .mov (1080p, full browser window).

Scene 1 — Open the app (5 sec)
  Navigate to https://plan2026.ca/login
  Pause so both "Continue with Google" and "Continue with Facebook" are visible.

Scene 2 — Start Facebook Login (10 sec)
  Click "Continue with Facebook".
  Show redirect to facebook.com consent screen.

Scene 3 — Consent (15 sec)
  Show requested permissions: public profile and email.
  Click Continue / OK to approve.

Scene 4 — Signed in (20 sec)
  Show redirect back to plan2026.ca/tasks.
  Point out the user's name in the header (from public_profile).
  Optionally open Settings to show account is active.

Scene 5 — Optional sign-out / sign-in again (15 sec)
  Sign out, return to /login, sign in with Facebook again to show repeat use.

Upload this video in Meta App Review → documents-web-1 (Supporting documentation).
You can also upload 01-login-page-with-facebook.png from this folder as a still image.
