Plan 2026 — Facebook Login test instructions for Meta App Review
================================================================

App URL (production):
  https://plan2026.ca/login

How to access the app:
  1. Open https://plan2026.ca/login in a desktop or mobile browser.
  2. No separate Plan 2026 account is required.
  3. Click the white "Continue with Facebook" button (below "Continue with Google").
  4. Sign in with your Facebook account and approve the requested permissions (public_profile, email).
  5. You will be redirected to https://plan2026.ca/tasks, signed in. Your name appears in the app header.

How the app uses Meta APIs:
  - We use Facebook Login (server-side OAuth via NextAuth) only for authentication.
  - Permissions requested: public_profile, email.
  - public_profile: display the user's name and profile picture in the app after sign-in.
  - email: identify the user account and associate tasks/plans with that account.
  - We do NOT post to Facebook, read friends, access Pages, or use any other Meta APIs.

Privacy and data deletion:
  - Privacy policy: https://plan2026.ca/privacy
  - Terms of service: https://plan2026.ca/terms
  - Users can request account/data deletion by email (details on the privacy page).

OAuth redirect URI configured in Meta app:
  https://plan2026.ca/api/auth/callback/facebook
